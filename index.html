F10 Station
Installation Guide

Step-by-step walkthrough for technicians installing the chargeFUZE F10 station.

5 Steps
~15 min
1 Person
01
PREP
Unbox & Verify Hardware
Open the F10 installation kit and verify all components before starting.
CONCEPT
→
REAL WORLD
☐ 2x Mounting Brackets
☐ 6x Screws + Washers
☐ Wall Anchors (25 lb rated)
☐ Paper Mounting Diagram
02
MARK & DRILL
Position Template, Mark Holes & Drill
Tape the included mounting diagram to the wall at your desired height. Mark through all 6 pre-cut holes, then drill pilot holes and insert anchors.
3 holes top bracket + 3 holes bottom = 6 total
Mark firmly with a pencil or awl
Zinc Anchor + Screw
Self-drilling zinc anchor for drywall. No pre-drilling needed. Drive anchor in, then screw through the bracket.
Tool: Phillips screwdriver or drill
View on Home Depot →
Triple Grip Anchor + Screw
Plastic self-drilling anchor rated for 46 lbs. Works in concrete, block, and wood. Pre-drill, tap anchor in, then drive the screw.
Tool: Drill + hammer
View on Home Depot →
⚠Mount at eye level in a high-traffic area. Near the host stand or bar is ideal.
03
MOUNT
Attach Brackets to Wall
Align the 2 mounting brackets over the anchors. Secure each with 3 screws and washers. Use a leveler to confirm they're straight before fully tightening.
FIELD VIDEO — Installing mounting brackets
3 screws per bracket — 6 total with washers
Level both brackets before final tightening
⚠A crooked station is an eyesore and looks unprofessional. Take the extra 30 seconds to level.
04
MOUNT
Hang Station & Secure
Lift the F10, align the rear slots over the bracket tabs, and shift downward to lock. Then install the security screws on each side to prevent removal.
FIELD VIDEO — Hanging & securing the F10
Align slots over tabs, shift down to lock
Install security screws with included bit
⚠Keep the security bit in your install kit at all times.
05
POWER
Plug In & Go Live
Route the power cable neatly to the nearest outlet and plug in. Confirm the screen powers on, test a charger eject, and snap a photo of the completed install.
This is what a clean install looks like.
✓ Screen powered on
✓ Charger ejects and reinserts
✓ Cable routed cleanly
✓ Install photo captured
⚠Always photo the completed install for your records and team verification.

Install Complete ⚡

The F10 is mounted, powered on, and ready to go. Clean work.
